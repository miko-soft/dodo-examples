dd-href

Handles link navigation and updates the browser history state.
Note: Clicking a dd-href element destroys the current controller — ddUNLISTEN() is invoked.

LINK1 -- href="/" - Reloads the page because the dd-href attribute is absent.
LINK2 -- href="/" dd-href - Navigates without reloading the page, acting as a single-page app link.
LINK3 -- href="" dd-href="pageURL" - Navigates without reloading. The URL is taken from the controller property.
LINK4 -- href="" dd-href="setUrl()" - Navigates without reloading. The URL is returned by a controller method.

history
