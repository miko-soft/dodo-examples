The this and $model prefixes can be omitted in dd-model, since it always refers to this.$model.
   dd-model="this.$model.myMdl_100" dd-setinitial="this.$model.myMdl_100"→
   dd-model="$model.myMdl_101" →
   dd-model="myMdl_102" →
