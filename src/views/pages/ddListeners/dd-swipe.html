dd-swipe

Detect a touch swipe gesture and execute a controller method. Direction is determined by comparing touchstart and touchend coordinates via a WeakMap. Both handlers are tracked in $dd.listeners so ddUNLISTEN() removes them on route change.

Options: --left   --right   --up   --down   (omit for any direction)

1. Image carousel — swipe left / right to change slide

‹ ›
Swipe left on the card (touch device) to go to the next slide, swipe right for previous.
Tip: open DevTools → toggle device toolbar → use pointer events to simulate touch swipes.
