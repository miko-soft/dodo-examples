dd-foreach (nested)

The dd-foreach is nested in another dd-foreach.
Used: $1{company.$0{field}} where $0 and $1 are interpolator marks defined by option --$0 and --$1
The "field" is array of strings.

Code


  <table>
    <tr>
      <th dd-foreach="fields --field">${field}</th>
    </tr>
    <tr dd-foreach="$model.companies --company --$1">
      <td dd-foreach="fields --field --$0">$1{company.$0{field}}</td>
    </tr>
  </table>
