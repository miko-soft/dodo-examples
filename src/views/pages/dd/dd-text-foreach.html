dd-text

Directive dd-text in combination with dd-foreach.

dd-text and dd-foreach in the same element

dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="animals[{{key}}]"


dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="animals.{{key}}"


dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="$$val"


dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="printAnimal(animals[{{key}}], {{key}})"


dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="printAnimal(animals.{{key}}, {{key}})"


dd-foreach="animals --val,key" dd-text="printAnimal($$val, {{key}})"


dd-text within dd-foreach

dd-foreach="animals --val,key" > dd-text="printAnimal($$val, {{key}})"


dd-foreach with template placeholders {{ ... }}

<p dd-foreach="animals --val,key">{{key}}. {{val}}</p>

{{key}}. {{val}}
